When would you want to use a remote repository rather than keeping all your work
local?

    You would use a remote repository when you want to be able to share files with and receive files from others,
    allowing you to collaborate more efficiently.

Why might you want to always pull changes manually rather than having Git
automatically stay up-to-date with your remote repository?

    You may not want to have all branches being developed by all collaborators
    on your project in your repository to keep it less cluttered.
